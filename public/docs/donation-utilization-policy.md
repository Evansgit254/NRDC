# Donation Utilization Policy
## Nutrition Relief & Development Centre (NRDC)

### 1. Commitment to Transparency
NRDC is committed to ensuring that every donation is used effectively and transparently to fulfill our mission.

### 2. Allocation of Funds
- **Direct Program Support:** At least 90% of all public donations are allocated directly to our nutrition, health, and food security programs.
- **Operational Costs:** No more than 10% of donations are used for administrative and fundraising expenses.

### 3. Monitoring and Evaluation
We conduct regular internal and external audits to monitor project implementation and ensure funds are reaching the intended beneficiaries.

### 4. Reporting
Impact reports and financial summaries are published annually to keep our donors informed about how their contributions are making a difference.
