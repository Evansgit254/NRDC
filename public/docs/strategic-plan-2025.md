# Strategic Plan 2025-2030
## Nutrition Relief & Development Centre (NRDC)

### 1. Executive Summary
NRDC is committed to addressing the immediate and long-term nutritional needs of refugees and displaced communities. This strategic plan outlines our roadmap for 2025-2030.

### 2. Vision and Mission
**Vision:** A world where displaced communities have the resources and support to thrive independently.
**Mission:** To provide nutrition, food security programs, capacity building, and community health support to refugees and displaced persons.

### 3. Strategic Goals
- **Goal 1:** Scale emergency nutrition interventions by 40%.
- **Goal 2:** Establish 50 new community-led sustainable farms.
- **Goal 3:** Strengthen maternal and child health programs.
- **Goal 4:** Achieve 100% transparency in donation utilization.

### 4. Implementation Framework
We will implement these goals through strategic partnerships, community empowerment, and data-driven interventions.
