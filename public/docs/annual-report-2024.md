# Annual Report 2024
## Nutrition Relief & Development Centre (NRDC)

### 1. Message from the Director
2024 was a year of resilience and significant impact. Thanks to our donors, we reached more families than ever before.

### 2. 2024 Impact Highlights
- **Lives Impacted:** 50,000+ individuals.
- **Meals Provided:** Over 1 million nutritious meals distributed.
- **Health Screenings:** 15,000+ individuals screened via mobile clinics.
- **Sustainable Farming:** 120+ community garden projects initiated.

### 3. Financial Overview
- **Total Revenue:** $1.2M (Donations and Grants)
- **Program Spending:** 92%
- **Administrative Costs:** 8%

### 4. Looking Ahead
In 2025, we aim to expand our reach to three new regions and introduce climate-resilient agricultural training.
