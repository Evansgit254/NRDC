# Donation Refund Policy
## Nutrition Relief & Development Centre (NRDC)

### 1. Policy Overview
At NRDC, we value the support of our donors. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds for donations may be processed.

### 2. Refund Requests
Donors may request a refund within 30 days of the donation date if:
- An error was made by NRDC in processing the donation.
- The donation was made in error (e.g., duplicate transaction).
- The donation was unauthorized or fraudulent.

### 3. Process for Requesting a Refund
To request a refund, please contact us at `nrdcofficial12@gmail.com` with your donation details, including the date, amount, and reference number.

### 4. Approval and Processing
All refund requests are reviewed within 5-7 business days. Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used.
